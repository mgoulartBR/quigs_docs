============================================
SYMFOOT v17 - FEATURE LIST
Football Manager for MSX/SymbOS
By BigFive
============================================

LEAGUE STRUCTURE
- 32 active teams in 4 divisions of 8
- 18 reserve teams (promotion/relegation pool)
- 14 rounds per season (home + away)
- Promotion: top 2 of each division go up
- Relegation: bottom 2 go down
- Div 4 bottom 2 swap with reserve pool
- External .SFL league files (editable)
- Real player data from CBF/Transfermarkt 2026

MATCH SIMULATION
- Force-based match engine (team strength + home advantage)
- Live match display showing all 4 divisions simultaneously
- 16 games per round with real-time score updates
- Goal events with scorer names and minute
- Progress bar for match time (1st/2nd half)

TEAM COLORS
- Each team has custom Paper (background) and Pen (text) colors
- 16-color SymbOS palette
- Colors loaded from .SFL file
- Colors follow teams through promotion/relegation

SQUAD MANAGEMENT
- 18 players per team (G, D, M, A positions)
- Squad display ordered by position (G→D→M→A)
- Player stats: Name, Age, Pts, Salary
- Player status: Starter (+), Suspended (SUS)
- Sell players (removed from squad)
- Buy players from transfer market

TRANSFER MARKET
- 3-6 random players available each round
- Players have position, age, pts, price
- Price based on pts and age (young = expensive)
- Bought player replaces weakest in squad
- Purchased player removed from market list

FORMATIONS
- 10 formations: 334, 343, 424, 433, 442, 451, 523, 532, 541, 631
- Auto-select best 11 based on formation
- Selectable via menu

FINANCES
- Team balance (income/expenses per round)
- Salary payments every round
- Ticket revenue from home games
- Sponsorship based on reputation
- Loan system (borrow/pay 500k)
- Debt with 5% interest per round

STADIUM
- Expandable (+5,000 capacity)
- Cost proportional to current size
- Ticket price adjustable (affects attendance)

ATTENDANCE
- Dynamic calculation based on:
  - Division factor (Div1=100%, Div4=40%)
  - Ticket price (higher = less fans)
  - Team reputation
  - Win streak (more wins = more fans)
  - Random variation (+/-10%)

PLAYER EVOLUTION
- Young players (<25): 30% chance to gain +1 pt per round
- Old players (>32): 20% chance to lose -1 pt per round
- Win bonus: 15% chance of +1 pt for starters
- Loss penalty: 10% chance of -1 pt for starters
- Retirement at age 42 (replaced by youth player)
- Annual aging: all players +1 year at season end

MANAGER CAREER
- Fired: 4+ consecutive losses
- Fired: position 7th/8th after round 10
- Fired: team relegated
- Invited: 5+ consecutive wins while 1st/2nd
- Invited: division champion at season end
- When fired: offered new team to manage
- When invited: accept/refuse offer from upper division

LEAGUE TABLE
- Manual bubble sort by Points → Wins → Draws
- 4 division tables (MList4, MList6, MList7, MList8)

SAVE/LOAD
- Binary save using WriteMemory/ReadMemory
- Fast: dumps 9216-byte Memory Blocks directly
- Saves: all player data, club data, calendar, reserves
- Incompatible with older save formats (version 3)

TECHNICAL
- Memory Blocks for player data (2x 9216 bytes)
- 16 getter/setter helper functions
- Data segment: ~11KB (within 16KB limit)
- Runs on MSX2/MSX2+ with SymbOS
- Z80 @ 3.58MHz, 128KB+ RAM
